The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite. It is about 384,000 kilometers away from Earth. The first humans to land on the Moon were Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin in 1969.

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun. It is known as the Red Planet due to its reddish appearance. NASA has sent several rovers to explore its surface.

The Milky Way is the galaxy that contains our Solar System. It is estimated to contain between 100 and 400 billion stars.
