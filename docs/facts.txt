The Eiffel Tower is located in Paris, France. It was built in 1889 and stands 330 meters tall. It was originally built as a temporary structure for the World's Fair.

The Great Wall of China stretches over 13,000 miles. It was built over many centuries by various Chinese dynasties. It is one of the greatest architectural feats in history.

The Amazon River is the largest river in the world by water volume. It flows through South America and is home to thousands of species of fish and wildlife.
